Tenacity

I’m the cofounder, designer, and developer of Tenacity — a responsive web app to help low-income high schoolers and those who support them navigate the complex path to college. See it in action


After an initial research and design phase, we settled on a few principles to guide the design of the product:

  • Only the right information at the right time
  • Focus on the now, but don’t lose sight of the big picture
  • Make it intuitive and fun to use
  • Offer explicit reasons to trust
  • Give parents and mentors well-timed tips
  • Be encouraging, honest, straightforward, and positive
  • Design mobile-first

The student signup process was designed to be as seamless as possible, while also welcoming the student and getting them fired up to achieve their college dreams.

I designed and built the flow and wrote the copy to be encouraging, honest, straightforward, and positive.

The mentor and administrator accounts make it easy to help students, even if as a mentor you're not a college expert yourself.
